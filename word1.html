Aesthetic

Meaning:

Aesthetic is used to talk about beauty or art, and people's appreciation of beautiful things

USES:

Designers use aesthetics to complement their designs' usability, and so enhance functionality with attractive layouts.

Synonyms:

Synonyms
Ornamental Artistic Pleasing
Beauty Beautifulness Elegance
Loveliness Prettiness Glamour

Antonyms:

Antonyms
Inelegant Inartistic Tasteless
Unattractive Ugly Unpleasing
