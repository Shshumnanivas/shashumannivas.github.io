Obstinate

Meaning:

1.Stubbornly refusing to change one’s opinion or chosen course of action, despite attempts to persuade one to do so.

2.Difficult to manage or control (can refer to things as well, like problems or stains).

USES:

1.He remained obstinate even after hearing everyone’s advice

2.She is too obstinate to admit she was wrong.
